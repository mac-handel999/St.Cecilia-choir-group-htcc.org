About Us





St.Cecilia Choir is a catholic choir group open and committed to all for choral activities and services while promoting catholic liturgy through singing.





St.Cecilia Choir choristers are professional musicians and choirmasters, emerging young artists and talented amateurs coming together to perform music designed to entertain and encourage our audiences and above all serve God.
We are truly a diverse mix that represents the Igbo culture.







Our Vision



St.Cecilia Choir is a catalyst for encouraging and nurturing hope
in the Catholic Church,
through good and inspiring Music.







Our Mission



St.Cecilia Choir is a group of musicians dedicated
to the excellent performance
of quality music to bring hope and joy to her listeners and parishioners.
